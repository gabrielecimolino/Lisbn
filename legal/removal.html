Content Removal Request

All of the content provided by Lisbn is fetched from other sources. This means that removing content from those other sources removes it from Lisbn. To request that content be removed from Lisbn, send your request to lisbn.project@gmail.com with the subject: Content Removal Request. We will attempt to forward your request to the content sources used by Lisbn.
